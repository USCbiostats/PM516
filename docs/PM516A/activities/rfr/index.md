# Red Flag Review

This week we will be playing Red Flag Review.

## About

For your assigned scenario, please identify the following:

1. What is the most important statistical "red flag" in this scenario?
2. Why is it problematic? (i.e., What is the consequence of this?)
3. What would you recommend doing instead?

Bonus points of note:

- Do you know the name of the statistical mistake?
- How would you bring this up to the investigator?

The class session will be structured as follows:

- Individual Review
- Team Huddle
- Group Presentation and Discussion
