# Week 6

## Statistical Mistakes

This week focuses on common statistical mistakes that can undermine otherwise well-intentioned research. As consultants, part of our role is not only to conduct analyses, but to recognize design flaws, misinterpretations, and analytic shortcuts that can compromise validity.

By the end of this week you will be able to:

1. Identify common statistical mistakes in study design and analysis.
2. Explain why these mistakes threaten analytic validity and propose appropriate remedies.
